✨ LightTours ✨

This app provides a way for you to see houses, parks, and other sites that have particularly impressive light displays this holiday season. Each location has a popup that details of those displays including number of lights and other notable features.

While “tacky lights displays” are perhaps the most classic example of the genre, and will certainly be included on the app, users will also be able to switch to display “classy lights displays” in order to see what might be termed more elegant examples.
